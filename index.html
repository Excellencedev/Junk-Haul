FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What items do you take?
We accept almost anything: furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, scrap metal, mattresses, tires, and more. If it fits in our truck, we haul it! For hazardous materials, please ask first — we'll point you in the right direction.
How much does junk removal cost?
Pricing depends on the volume and type of junk you need removed. We offer free, no-obligation estimates — just send us photos or give us a call. We're typically 20-30% cheaper than other Austin junk removal companies with no hidden fees.
Do you offer same-day service?
Yes! Call us before 2 PM and we'll do our best to get you on the schedule the same day. Same-day service is available in most of our service areas. Give us a call at (512) 436-6298 to check availability.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind. We treat your property with the same care we'd treat our own. Feel free to ask for our license and insurance details.
Do you recycle or donate items?
Yes! We prioritize eco-friendly disposal. Usable items are donated to local Austin charities, electronics are recycled properly, and scrap metal is processed at recycling facilities. We're committed to keeping Central Texas green.
Do you offer dump trailer rentals?
Yes! We offer both daily and weekly dump trailer rentals. We deliver the trailer to your location, you fill it up at your own pace, and we pick it up when you're done. Perfect for DIY renovation projects, landscaping jobs, and large cleanouts.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Austin and over 15 surrounding cities including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Kyle, Leander, Buda, Bastrop, Killeen, Temple, Belton, San Marcos, New Braunfels, and Harker Heights. If you're not sure if we serve your area, just give us a call!
