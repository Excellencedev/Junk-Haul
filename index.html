Frequently Asked Questions

What items do you take?

We accept almost anything: furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, scrap metal, mattresses, tires, and more. If it fits, we haul it! For hazardous materials, please ask first.

How much does junk removal cost?

Pricing depends on the volume and type of junk. We offer free, no-obligation estimates — just send us photos or give us a call. We're typically 20-30% cheaper than other Austin junk removal companies.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! Call us before 2 PM and we'll do our best to get you on the schedule the same day. Same-day service is available in most areas.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind. We treat your property with the same care we'd treat our own.

Do you recycle or donate items?

Yes! We prioritize eco-friendly disposal. Usable items are donated to local charities, and scrap metal is recycled. We're committed to keeping Austin green.

Do you offer dump trailer rentals?

Yes! We offer daily and weekly dump trailer rentals. We deliver the trailer to you, you fill it up, and we pick it up. Perfect for DIY projects.
